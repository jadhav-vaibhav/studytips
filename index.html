Planning of study studies

When students sit to study, 'what to study?' It takes a lot of time to decide. It takes time to choose the subject. Don't like English, easy Marathi, don't like geography, the subjects are divided in this way. Well, if you choose a subject, it takes time to choose a lesson and till then, TV. A favorite program appears on top. So students should plan their studies. I should record what I am going to study tomorrow, what I am going to read in the study calendar (Planner) while sleeping tonight. This is called directional study.

Tips to make plan

  • Different types of diaries, study planners, calendars are available in the market. Finding a diary that works for you and then sticking to it is the first step in planning your study
  • Make a plan of exactly what and how much you want to study in the scheduled time and then start studying.
  • Make a time table and try to follow it.

Why is study time wasted?

  • Storing study material anywhere and finding it
  • Discussing unnecessary things other than studying.
  • Spending time thinking about what to study as there is no study plan
  • Not having books, notebooks in the study area and sitting looking for where they are kept.

What things save time?

  • Planning of study in written form (Study Schedule).
  • Every night due to revision of studies done during the day.
  • When you are tired study Memory related subject or skill related subject
  • Informing all friends, family about your study times...
